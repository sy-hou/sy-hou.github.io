The Verdict
SHOULD YOU JOIN?
You already have Wednesday gym access for free. Here's the honest breakdown.
Verdict: Yes — but only from Phase 2
✓ Recommended from Week 9

For Phase 1 (Weeks 1–8), your college session covers what you need. Your Monday and Saturday sessions are bodyweight and court-based — no gym required. Save your money for now.

From Week 9 onwards, a membership becomes genuinely worth it because:

  • You need heavy leg days (squats, deadlifts, split squats) that require a barbell — this is the #1 driver of your vertical jump and on-court explosiveness
  • Your college session is only 1 hour — not enough volume to split upper/lower as your training increases
  • At 64kg, consistent resistance work is the fastest way to add the functional mass you need for physical play
  • Tuesday leg sessions + Wednesday upper sessions is the ideal split, and you can't do both in one college session

Look for gyms with a month-to-month contract — no need to lock in long term at first. Many gyms offer student discounts too, so ask specifically.

Bottom line: Phase 1 = college gym only. Phase 2+ = add your own membership, ~1–2 sessions per week on top of Wednesday. The cost pays for itself in athletic performance.
